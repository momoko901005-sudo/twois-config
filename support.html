The World Only I See - Support

只有我看见的世界 (The World Only I See) is an iOS AR nurturing game where you discover mysterious creatures hidden in the real world, build bonds with them through daily interactions, and watch them evolve into unique forms.

Features

  • AR Discovery: Use your camera to find anomalies in the real world
  • Character Nurturing: Build intimacy and watch characters evolve through multiple stages
  • AI Dialogue: Have meaningful conversations with your characters powered by AI
  • Weather & Health Integration: Characters react to real-world weather and your daily activity
  • Character Diary: Characters write diary entries reflecting your shared experiences

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I discover new characters?

A: Use the AR scan feature to explore your surroundings. Different characters may appear in different environments and conditions.

Q: How do characters evolve?

A: Characters evolve as your intimacy level increases through daily interactions, chatting, feeding, and spending time together. Each character has multiple evolution stages.

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?

A: The core game works offline. An internet connection is only needed for AI dialogue and weather features.

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes. All game data is stored locally on your device. AI chat features require your explicit consent, and health data never leaves your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: How do I reset my game data?

A: Go to Settings within the app and select "Reset All Data." Please note this action is irreversible.

Q: The AR feature is not working. What should I do?

A: Make sure you have granted camera permission in iOS Settings → Privacy → Camera. AR features require a device with ARKit support.

Q: Can I switch my character's displayed form?

A: Yes! Once a character has evolved, you can tap on unlocked evolution stages in the character detail view to switch their displayed form.

常见问题

Q：如何发现新角色？

A：使用 AR 扫描功能探索你的周围环境。不同的角色可能在不同的环境和条件下出现。

Q：角色如何进化？

A：通过每日互动、聊天、喂食和陪伴来提升亲密度，亲密度达到一定程度后角色会进化。每个角色都有多个进化阶段。

Q：应用需要联网吗？

A：核心游戏功能可离线运行。仅 AI 对话和天气功能需要网络连接。

Q：我的数据安全吗？

A：安全。所有游戏数据都存储在您的设备本地。AI 聊天功能需要您的明确同意，健康数据永远不会离开您的设备。详情请查看隐私政策。

Q：如何重置游戏数据？

A：在应用内进入 设置，选择"重置所有数据"。请注意此操作不可撤销。

Q：AR 功能无法使用怎么办？

A：请确认已在 iOS 设置 → 隐私 → 相机 中授予相机权限。AR 功能需要支持 ARKit 的设备。

Q：可以切换角色的显示形态吗？

A：可以！角色进化后，在角色详情页点击已解锁的进化阶段即可切换显示形态。
