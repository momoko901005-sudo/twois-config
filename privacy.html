The World Only I See - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to "The World Only I See" (只有我看见的世界)! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

App Name: 只有我看见的世界 (The World Only I See)
Platform: iOS

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • User Profile: Your in-app nickname
  • Chat Messages: Text messages you send to characters via the AI chat feature
  • Game Data: Character progress, intimacy levels, evolution stages, collected items
  • App Preferences: Sound settings, notification preferences, AI model selection

2.2 Health Data (Optional)

If you grant permission, we access your health data through Apple HealthKit:

  • Step Count: Used for in-game interactions (e.g., character diary context, step-based rewards)
  • Sleep Data: Used for character interactions and resonance mechanics

Important:

  • Health data is never sent to external servers
  • All health data stays on your device
  • We do not sell or share health data with any third parties
  • You can revoke access anytime in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices

2.3 Location Data (Optional)

  • Approximate Location: Used to fetch current weather conditions via Apple WeatherKit
  • Location accuracy is set to kilometer-level (not precise GPS)
  • Location data is not stored or transmitted to any third party
  • You can revoke access anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services

2.4 Camera Access (Optional)

  • Camera: Used for the AR scanning feature (discovering anomalies in augmented reality)
  • Camera feed is processed locally on your device using ARKit
  • No images or video are captured, stored, or transmitted

3. Third-Party AI Services

Important: This app uses third-party AI services to generate character dialogue and diary content. The app will ask for your explicit consent before sending any data to these services.

3.1 What Data Is Sent

When you use the AI chat or diary generation features, the following data may be sent to a third-party AI service:

  • Your chat messages (text you type to the character)
  • Character personality and setting information
  • Interaction context: intimacy level, daily step count, current weather, time of day

3.2 Who Receives the Data

Depending on your AI model selection in Settings, data is sent to one of the following providers:

  • Moonshot AI (Kimi): Operated by Beijing Moonshot Technology Co., Ltd. (月之暗面科技有限公司)
    Privacy Policy: https://www.moonshot.cn/privacy
  • Zhipu AI (GLM): Operated by Beijing Zhipu Huazhang Technology Co., Ltd. (北京智谱华章科技有限公司)
    Privacy Policy: https://www.zhipuai.cn/privacy

3.3 Purpose

  • Data is used solely to generate character dialogue and diary content
  • Data is not used for advertising, user profiling, or any other purpose

3.4 Your Control

  • AI features require your explicit consent before activation
  • You can disable AI features anytime in Settings → AI Dialogue
  • When AI is disabled, no data is sent to any third-party service
  • You can clear chat history in the app at any time

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide and improve the App experience:

  • Core Gameplay: Track character progress, manage evolution, record interactions
  • AI Features: Generate personalized character dialogue and diary entries (with your consent)
  • Weather Integration: Display weather-influenced character interactions
  • AR Experience: Discover and collect anomalies in augmented reality

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • This includes: user profile, character data, chat history, diary entries, and game progress
  • We do not operate our own servers for data storage

5.2 Third-Party AI Processing

  • Data sent to AI services is processed according to their respective privacy policies
  • We do not control or have access to data once it is received by the AI service providers

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your data.

We only share data in these limited circumstances:

  • AI Service Providers: Chat messages and context data are sent to Moonshot AI or Zhipu AI (only with your explicit consent, as described in Section 3)
  • Apple Services: WeatherKit (weather data), HealthKit (health data), ARKit (AR processing) — all processed per Apple's privacy policy
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law or to protect our rights

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • AI Consent: You must explicitly agree before any AI features are enabled
  • Disable AI: Settings → AI Dialogue → Turn Off (stops all third-party data sharing)
  • Clear Chat History: Clear conversation records within the chat interface
  • Delete All Data: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings to permanently delete all app data
  • Revoke HealthKit Access: iOS Settings → Privacy → Health
  • Revoke Location Access: iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services
  • Revoke Camera Access: iOS Settings → Privacy → Camera

8. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information through the AI chat feature, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Showing an in-app notification when applicable
  • Posting the updated policy on this page

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

1. 简介

欢迎使用《只有我看见的世界》！我们尊重您的隐私并致力于保护您的个人数据。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您使用本应用时的信息。

2. 我们收集的信息

2.1 您提供的信息

  • 用户资料：应用内昵称
  • 聊天消息：您通过AI对话功能发送给角色的文字消息
  • 游戏数据：角色进度、亲密度、进化阶段、收集的物品
  • 应用偏好：音效设置、通知偏好、AI模型选择

2.2 健康数据（可选）

如果您授予权限，我们会通过 Apple HealthKit 访问您的健康数据：

  • 步数：用于游戏互动（角色日记、步数奖励等）
  • 睡眠数据：用于角色互动和共鸣机制

重要说明：健康数据从不发送到外部服务器，仅保存在您的设备上。

2.3 位置数据（可选）

  • 仅用于通过 Apple WeatherKit 获取当前天气
  • 使用千米级精度（非精确GPS定位）
  • 位置数据不会被存储或传输给第三方

2.4 相机（可选）

  • 用于AR扫描功能（探索增强现实中的异常信号）
  • 相机画面仅在设备本地通过ARKit处理
  • 不会拍摄、存储或传输任何图片或视频

3. 第三方AI服务

重要说明：本应用使用第三方AI服务生成角色对话和日记内容。在发送任何数据前，应用会征求您的明确同意。

发送的数据：

  • 您输入的聊天消息
  • 角色性格和设定信息
  • 互动上下文：亲密度等级、当日步数、天气、时间段

数据接收方：

  • 月之暗面科技有限公司（Moonshot AI / Kimi）
    隐私政策：https://www.moonshot.cn/privacy
  • 北京智谱华章科技有限公司（智谱AI / GLM）
    隐私政策：https://www.zhipuai.cn/privacy

您的控制权：

  • AI功能需要您的明确同意才会启用
  • 您可以随时在 设置 → AI对话 中关闭AI功能
  • 关闭后不会向任何第三方发送数据
  • 您可以随时清除聊天记录

4. 数据存储

  • 所有游戏数据本地存储在您的设备上
  • 我们不运营自有服务器存储用户数据

5. 数据分享

我们不出售您的数据。

  • AI服务商：经您明确同意后，聊天消息和上下文数据发送至 Moonshot AI 或智谱 AI
  • Apple 服务：WeatherKit（天气）、HealthKit（健康）、ARKit（AR）
